Raw Salad
English summary
Food historians say the Romans ate mixed greens and dressing, and the Babylonians were known to have dressed greens with oil and vinegar two thousand years ago.[3][4][5] In his 1699 book, Acetaria: A Discourse on Sallets, John Evelyn attempted with little success to encourage his fellow Britons to eat fresh salad greens.[6] Royalty dabbled in salads: Mary, Queen of Scots, ate boiled celery root over salad covered with creamy mustard dressing, truffles, chervil, and slices of hard-boiled eggs.[4]
The United States popularized salads in the late 19th century and other regions of the world adopted them throughout the second half of the 20th century. From Europe and the Americas to China, Japan, and Australia, premade salads are sold in supermarkets, at restaurants (restaurants will often have a "Salad Bar" laid out with salad-making ingredients, which the customers will use to put together their salad) and at fast food chains. Access to public data in Poland is getting better, but still lacks the standards of their presentation. Raw Salad answers the need for a fast, efficient and standardized access to public data. Salad Raw Data Viewer is a browser, allowing easy access to public data. The main subject of the efforts of developers Raw Salad is to create an efficient and transparent system of movement in the thicket of data. By using modern information technology users get the tool to easily choose the data they need and adjust output to meet their needs.

Raw Salad offers a unified workflow. Regardless of the data structure and the contents viewing data in the Raw Salad looks the same. In the future, this will create a unified navigation system within the whole public data.
An important element is the functionality of Raw Salad Rich download, allowing each stage to work with data download interesting user data in a form which facilitates their further processing in any environment and placing them in a spreadsheet, email, blog, etc.

From the perspective of software developers the most important element is the Raw Salad’s public application programming interface (Eng: API Application Programming Interface). With API any developer creating a web page, or visualization application for mobile phone, can use data collected by us (eg EU budget funds). She/he will be able to download them directly from the place where they are stored – in the open data base.
